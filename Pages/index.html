About Us

Genebria, is a modern streetwear brand inspired by the legacy and resilience of Liberia. Founded on the belief that fashion can tell stories, GENBERIA weaves together culture, struggle, and resilience into every piece. Our name is a fusion of "Generation" and "Liberia", symbolizing the pride of a people who’ve endured, evolved, and continue to rise. Our debut collection, "Since 1822," honors the past while shaping the future, blending heritage with contemporary design. Each piece is crafted with purpose, echoing our belief that fashion is a powerful form of storytelling. Resilient Forever is more than a slogan — it’s who we are. Join us. Wear the story. Discover GENBERIA.
